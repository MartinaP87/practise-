Our major will talk about the our territory and it’s protection plans, after the speech there will also be the chance to have an open dibate, so bring your ideas to improve the envoirement!

Arboretum And Giants Trecking

You can visit the most important Italian collection of plants grown for scientific and experimental porpuses. There are about 5000 speciman devided in over 700 species of plants and trees. Keep walking and you’ll be able to see The Giants! The tallest trees in Italy, 60 metres height of nature!

For those who can still walk and for the late arrivals the haunt for chestnuts begins! Our guide will walk you to the forest section where you’ll find plenty of them, take it easy, there’s enough for everyone! This time is not about how many you can spot, it’s about how many you can carry!

Now that we drunk and eat enugh it’s time to dance it off in the best old fashion way! Our local band will showcase their repertory playing from calssic traditional music till more recent times banger tunes.
